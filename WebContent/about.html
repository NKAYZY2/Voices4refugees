About Us



Voices for Refugees


Voices for Refugees is a focus group striving to raise awareness about refugee issues and crises across the globe. We are dedicated to achieve equality for all. We are a group of students who are the microphone to those who aren't heard, speaking with those who are amidst the violence scourging their homes.


We speak out for those who are wrongly incarcerated for believing in their rights. We are a voice that listens.
